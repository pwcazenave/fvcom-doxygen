247  SUBROUTINE wqmpara
248 !=============================================================================!
249 ! !
250 ! This subroutine provides values of all parameters used in !
251 ! the conventional eutrophication water quality model in !
252 ! the Satilla River, Georgia. The water quality model in !
253 ! the Satilla River includes eight variables. They are: !
254 ! (1) Dissolved Oxygen (DO), mg O2/l; !
255 ! (2) Carbonaceous Biochemical Oxygen Demand (CBOD), mg C/l; !
256 ! (3) Phytoplankton (PHYT), mg C/l; !
257 ! (4) Ammonia Nitrogen (NH3), mg N/l; !
258 ! (5) Nitrate Nitrogen (NO3), mg N/l; !
259 ! (6) Organic Nitrogen (ON), mg N/l; !
260 ! (7) Orthophosphorus (or Inorganic Phosphorus, OPO4), mg P/l; !
261 ! (8) Organic Phosphorus (OP), mg P/l. !
262 ! !
263 ! The water quality model was separated into water column and sediment !
264 ! two layers. At the sediment layer, due to anaerobic condition, no !
265 ! phytoplankton variable available. !
266 ! !
267 ! This code is origionaly written by L. Zheng in March, 2000 for ECOM, !
268 ! and rewriten by J. Qi for FVCOM in 2002 and 2004. !
269 !=============================================================================!
270 
271 !-------------------------------------------------------------------------
272 ! The definition for each parameter in water column is:
273 !
274 ! K_deox: Deoxygenation rate at 20 degree, 0.16-0.21, (day^-1);
275 ! K_nitr: Nitrification rate at 20 degree, 0.09-0.13, (day^-1);
276 ! K_resp: Phytoplankton respiration rate at 20 degree, (day^-1);
277 ! K_resp1: Bacterial respiration rate, 0.8 (uM/h);
278 ! K_deni: Denitrification rate at 20 degree, (day^-1);
279 ! K_grow: Optimum phytoplankton growth rate at 20 degree, (day^-1);
280 ! K_mort: The Mortality rate of phytoplankton at 20 degree, (day^-1);
281 ! K_mine1: Organic nitrogen mineralization at 20 degree, (day^-1);
282 ! K_mine2: Organic phosphorus mineralization at 20 degree, (day^-1);
283 ! KBOD: Half-saturation constant for oxygen limitation, (mg O2/l);
284 ! KNITR: Half-saturation constant for oxygen limitation, (mg O2/l);
285 ! KmN: Half-saturation constant for uptake of inorganic
286 ! nitrogen, (ug N/l);
287 ! KmP: Half-saturation constant for uptake of inorganic
288 ! phosphorus, (ug P/l);
289 ! KNO3: Half-saturation constant for oxygen limitation, (mg O2/l);
290 ! KmPC: Half-saturation constant of phytoplankton limitation of
291 ! phosphorus recycle, (mg C/l);
292 ! Temp_reae: Temperature coefficient of reaeration;
293 ! Temp_deox: Temperature coefficient of deoxygenation;
294 ! Temp_nitr: Temperature coefficient of nitrification;
295 ! Temp_resp: Temperature coefficient of phytoplankton respiration;
296 ! Temp_deni: Temperature coefficient of denitrification, 1.045-1.08;
297 ! Temp_grow: Temperature coefficient of optimum growth;
298 ! Temp_mort: Temperature coefficient of phytoplankton mortality;
299 ! Temp_mine1:Temperature coefficient of nitrogen mineralization;
300 ! Temp_mine2:Temperature coefficient of phosphorus mineralization;
301 ! Temp_sod: Temperature coefficient of SOD;
302 ! WS2: Organic matter sinking velocity, (m/day);
303 ! WS3: Phytoplankton settling velocity, (m/day);
304 ! FD2: Fraction of dissolved CBOD;
305 ! FD6: Fraction of dissolved organic nitrogen;
306 ! FD8: Fraction of dissolved organic phosphorus;
307 ! FON: Fraction of dead and respired phytoplankton recycled to
308 ! the organic nitorgen pool;
309 ! FOP: Fraction of dead and respired phytoplankton recycled to
310 ! the organic phosphorus pool;
311 ! SOD: Sediment Oxygen Demand at 20 degree, 0.2-0.4, (g/m^2.day);
312 ! Time_U: Time (in hour) of sunrise;
313 ! Time_D: Time (in hour) of sunset;
314 ! Solar_S: Optimum solar radiation rate, (langleys/day);
315 ! Solar_A: Total daily solar radiation, (langleys/day);
316 ! Ratio_NC: Ratio of nitrogen to carbon in phytoplankton,
317 ! 16/106 by Redfield ratio, (mg N/mg C);
318 ! Ratio_PC: Ratio of phosphorus to carbon in phytoplankton,
319 ! 1/106 by Redfield ratio, (mg P/mg C);
320 !
321 ! The definition for each parameter in the sediment layer is:
322 !
323 ! KB_ds: Organic Carbon (as CBOD) decomposition rate at
324 ! 20 degree, (day^-1);
325 ! KB_pzd: Anaerobic algal decomposition rate at 20 degree, (day^-1);

327 ! KB_ond: Organic nitrogen decomposition rate at 20 degree, (day^-1);
328 ! KB_opd: Organic phosphorus decomposition rate at 20 degree, (day^-1);
329 ! TempB_ds: Temperature coefficient of organic carbon decomposition;
330 ! TempB_pzd: Temperature coefficient of anaerobic algal decomposition;
331 ! TempB_deni:Temperature coefficient of denitrification;
332 ! TempB_ond: Temperature coefficient of organic nitrogen decomposition;

334 ! FDB2: Fraction of dissolved CBOD in sediment;
335 ! FDB4: Fraction of dissolved NH3 in sediment;

337 ! FDB6: Fraction of dissolved ON in sediment;
