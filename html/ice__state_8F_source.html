13 ! !DESCRIPTION:
14 !
15 ! Primary state variables in various configurations
16 ! Note: other state variables are at the end of this...
17 ! The primary state variable names are:
18 !-------------------------------------------------------------------
19 ! for each category aggregated over units
20 ! categories
21 !-------------------------------------------------------------------
22 ! aicen(i,j,n) aice(i,j) ---
23 ! vicen(i,j,n) vice(i,j) m
24 ! vsnon(i,j,n) vsno(i,j) m
25 ! eicen(i,j,k) eice(i,j) J/m$^2$
26 ! esnon(i,j,n) esno(i,j) J/$m^2$
27 ! Tsfcn(i,j,n) Tsfc(i,j) deg
28 !
29 ! Area is dimensionless because aice is the fractional area
30 ! (normalized so that the sum over all categories, including open
31 ! water, is 1.0). That is why vice/vsno have units of m instead of
32 ! m$^3$, and eice/esno have units of J/m$^2$ instead of J.
33 !
34 ! Variable names follow these rules:
35 !
36 ! (1) For 3D variables (indices i,j,n), write 'ice' or 'sno' or
37 ! 'sfc' and put an 'n' at the end.
38 ! (2) For 2D variables (indices i,j) aggregated over all categories,
39 ! write 'ice' or 'sno' or 'sfc' without the 'n'.
40 ! (3) For 2D variables (indices i,j) associated with an individual
41 ! category, write 'i' or 's' instead of 'ice' or 'sno' and put an 'n'
42 ! at the end: e.g. hin, hsn. These are not declared here
43 ! but in individual modules (e.g., ice\_therm\_vertical).
44 !
